MIT EDITORIAL BOARD

Welcome to the MIT Editorial Board, the creative hub and driving force behind the Manipal Institute of Technology's official student body and yearbook committee.Comprising dedicated individuals passionate about storytelling, design, and capturing the vibrant spirit of our institute, we take pride in shaping the narrative that defines our community. From selecting compelling themes to curating content that reflects the diversity of experiences, achievements, and culture at MIT, our editorial team works collaboratively with writers, photographers, and designers to produce a publication that stands as a testament to the memorable moments of each academic year. Join us in the journey of preserving and celebrating the essence of Manipal Institute of Technology through the power of words and images.
